## Recording Recommendations

### Preparation
1. **Practice 3 times minimum** with timer
2. **Export slides to PDF** to avoid software crashes
3. **Test screen recording** before final take
4. **Have water nearby** (you'll be talking for 20 minutes straight)

### Technical Setup
- **Resolution:** 1080p minimum
- **Software:** OBS Studio (free, reliable)
- **Audio:** Use close mic, reduce background noise
- **Camera:** Optional webcam in corner (adds personality)

### During Recording
- **Speak to camera**, not screen
- **Use laser pointer/cursor** when referencing diagrams
- **Pause 2-3 seconds** between slide transitions
- **Vary your pitch** to avoid monotone
- **Smile** at key achievements

### Post-Production
- **Trim silence** at start/end
- **Check audio levels** (normalize if needed)
- **Add title card** with slide number (optional but professional)
- **Upload as Unlisted** to YouTube
- **Test link** in incognito mode before submitting
